Movies, like memories, change with us.

When we revisit a film years later, we're not just rewatching the same story — we're bringing new eyes, new context, and often, a new version of ourselves.

Rewatching isn’t redundancy. It’s discovery.

---

### Why it matters

- You notice subtle storytelling cues you missed.
- Emotions hit differently depending on life stage.
- Art evolves when your lens evolves.

> “The best movies don’t change — you do.”  
> _— A Film Lover_

So the next time someone says, “you’ve already seen that,”  
just smile and say:  
**“Not really.”**
