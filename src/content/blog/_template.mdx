## 🚀 Introduction

Write an engaging introduction that:
- Hooks the reader with a relatable problem or interesting fact
- Clearly states what the post will cover
- Explains why the reader should care
- Sets expectations for what they'll learn

**Pro tip:** Use the first paragraph to make a promise to your reader about what value they'll get.

---

## 🎯 Main Content Section

### Subsection with Clear Headings

Use clear, descriptive headings that help readers scan your content. Break up long sections with:

- **Bullet points** for easy scanning
- **Bold text** for emphasis
- `Inline code` for technical terms
- <span className="highlight">Custom highlights</span> for important concepts

- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Internal links to other posts when relevant
